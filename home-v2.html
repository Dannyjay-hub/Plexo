What is Plexo?

Plexo is a blockchain based credentialing platform aimed at helping students share their verified Educational Credentials and Documents with Potential Employers or Educational Institutions.

Plexo aims to reduce the number of forged records circulating the World Of Work by ensuring credibility and accountability is attached to every document.
